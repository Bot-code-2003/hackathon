XR MONK'S

"Hackathon and Bootcamp"

The XR Monk Hackathon and Bootcamp is an intensive 3-day event focused on Augmented and Virtual Reality (AR/VR). It combines a professional-led bootcamp, teaching the fundamentals of AR/VR, with a hackathon where participants apply their new skills to innovate in the field. Perfect for enthusiasts looking to dive into AR/VR technology.
